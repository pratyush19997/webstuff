How to make sunny side up

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • common sense

Method

  1. put butter in a pan pre-heated on medium-high
  2. crack eggs and pour egg in the pan
  3. wait for eggs to be cooked, there are different types of eggs:
    • cat
    • dog
  4. when eggs are cooked, put them on plate
  5. enjoy
